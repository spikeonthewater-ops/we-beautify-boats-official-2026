Welcome to We Beautify Boats! We deliver premium, assessment-first marine detailing and maintenance across Ontario's freshwater fleet
. Our services are built on clearly defined, evidence-based standards rather than guesswork, ensuring your vessel is protected, beautiful, and ready for the water
. Below is the complete directory of our service areas, tiered levels, and community events to structure your new website.
1. Deck Wash Services
Tiered deck washing is engineered to preserve non-skid performance and safely reset surfaces without over-cleaning
.
Level 1 (Basic Detail): Routine maintenance washdown for light surface dirt; no surface restoration
.
Level 2 (Conditioning Detail): Enhanced cleaning targeting moderate grime, bonded body oils, and vinyl/window conditioning
.
Level 3 (Deep Reset): Intensive inch-by-inch scrubbing, including deck lockers, canvas, and teak cleaning
.
Level 4 (Intervention): Disaster-level recovery to strip moss, mold, tree sap, and organic fallout from neglected decks
.

2. Interior Detailing
Comprehensive interior care ranging from routine wipe-downs to full environmental remediation to restore a fresh, healthy cabin
.
Level 1 (Basic): Routine cabin care, dusting, and vacuuming for a tidy, guest-ready space
.
Level 2 (Reset): Deep cleaning refresh, detailing cupboards, drawers, windows, and hard-to-reach areas
.
Level 3 (Sanitization): Deep steam-cleaned sanitization for carpets, upholstery, bilges, and appliances
.
Level 4 (Intervention): Maximum abatement for severe mold, pests, flooding, and odor using ozone treatment
.

3. Hull Wash Services
Eco-friendly topside cleaning designed to remove scum lines and buildup without compromising the hull's surface integrity
.
Level 1 (Brush Wash): Basic brush and rinse to remove surface salt, dust, and light marine growth
.
Level 2 (Hand Wash): Targeted scrubbing to remove green slime and grime specifically along the foul line
.
Level 3 (Decontamination): Intensive deep wash to remove yellow/brown tannin staining and heavy oxidation
.

4. Bottom Prep
Critical underwater surface preparation required for maintaining hydrodynamics and ensuring optimal coating adhesion
.
Level 1 (Basic Prep): Light fouling and slime removal using soft scrapers; no sanding
.
Level 2 (Standard Prep): Moderate fouling removal and scuff sanding to promote antifouling paint adhesion
.
Level 3 (Aggressive Prep): Heavy-duty prep to sand away marine growth, barnacles, and flaking paint
.
Level 4 (Complete Reset): Complete bare gelcoat reset using the Jango 1000 Class 4 laser system for zero-waste, dustless paint removal
.

5. Deck & Hull Polishing
Scaled surface correction techniques designed to safely level gelcoat and restore showroom brilliance
.
Level 1 (Basic Refresh): Light, single-stage mid-season maintenance polish for well-kept surfaces
.
Level 2 (Correction Lite): Machine polishing to remove light oxidation and minor surface scuffs
.
Level 3 (Correction Standard): Multi-step correction to restore gloss and color to chalky, moderately oxidized gelcoat
.
Level 4 (Correction Heavy): Intensive, multi-stage compound correction for severe oxidation, staining, and neglect
.
Level 5 (Correction Extreme): Staged wet sanding to physically level the gelcoat, resulting in a flawless, mirror-like finish
.

6. Protections
Advanced surface shields that lock in your detailing investment and protect against UV rays, salt, and oxidation
.
Marine Wax: Classic, affordable seasonal protection for a smooth, glossy finish
.
SiO2 Coating: Permanon nano-engineered coating for extreme water beading and dirt resistance without waxing
.
SpikePT: Ultimate Teflon-based (PTFE) fusion coating for long-lasting, maintenance-free durability
.
Antifouling & Epoxy Barrier: Essential underwater defense systems against marine growth drag and osmotic blistering
