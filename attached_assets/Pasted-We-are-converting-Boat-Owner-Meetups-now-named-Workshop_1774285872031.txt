### The Level 10 (L10) Marine Meeting Structure
To fulfill your goal of integrating Level 10 meetings into these modules, here is the standardized L10 format tailored to the We Beautify Boats "Kaizen" and "Evidence-Based Action" values, meant to be held during the final segment of each session to foster open communication and feedback:
1.  **Segue / Wins (5 mins):** Share a recent detailing victory or positive client feedback.
2.  **Scorecard (5 mins):** Review standard times for tasks (e.g., timing a Level 1 Deck Wash).
3.  **Headlines (5 mins):** Quick updates on marina regulations, environmental compliance, or crew logistics.
4.  **To-Do List (5 mins):** Review any carry-over action items from previous training.
5.  **IDS - Identify, Discuss, Solve (20 mins):** The core of the L10. Participants bring up specific pre-service conditions (e.g., failing antifoul, heavy oxidation, surface memory) and the group discusses and solves the approach using the Level 1-5 service matrix.
6.  **Conclude (5 mins):** Recap the solved issues and rate the meeting.
