Web Copy for Event Pages
1. The Marina Pro-Series
Target Audience: Staff & Crews (Professional) Description: Built for efficiency, workflow optimization, and crew leadership. This event is designed for professional marina teams and detailing crews serving multiple vessels at scale. We dive into the logistics of marine detailing, time management, and the leadership required to maintain high service standards without avoidable rework
. Your staff will learn to read surfaces before a buffer ever touches a boat, implementing systematic quality control and our Predictive Buffer Workflow to eliminate guesswork.
2. Yacht Club "Owner's Day"
Target Audience: Private Owners (Owner) Description: Bring 30 years of recreational marine expertise directly to your yacht club members. This workshop focuses on practical DIY skills, tool safety, and seasonal maintenance fundamentals. Owners will learn the critical difference between cosmetic appearance and true asset preservation
, as well as how to perform safe, eco-conscious maintenance that protects Ontario's freshwater lakes
. Take the knowledge home to extend the life of your own boat, knowing exactly when to use routine care and when to call a professional.
3. The Restoration Masterclass
Target Audience: Advanced Technicians (Advanced) Description: For experienced hands ready to operate at the highest level. This highly technical masterclass covers Level 5 surface correction, including the precise stages of wet sanding to safely level heavily degraded gelcoat
. We will also cover spot repairs and the application of Spike’s PT (PPS Technology®) to fuse long-lasting PTFE protection to marine surfaces
. This is an abrasive, direct route to pulling the beautiful finish back out of the fiberglass, transforming technicians into true craftsmen
.
